8. MASTER THESE SPELLINGS

Sometimes JAMB tests students on spelling of words, just make sure you take note of the right spelling giving below.

It is not easy to ______ jobs sweeping in the streets and on campus.

A. maneuver. B. manouever. C. manuver. D. maneuvar

(Maneuver)

 

The defendant claimed he had been ______ into making a statement

A. coarced. B. coaxed. C. coarsed. D. coerced

(Coerced)

 

We all ______ sigh of relief when. We heard he had won at the polls.

A. heeved. B. hived. C. heaved. D. heved

(heaved)
