Kinetic theory of an ideal gas

NB: The pressure of the gas arises due to the molecules bombarding the walls of its container. 

In order to derive an expression for pressure of a gas, the following assumption will be made. 

  • 1. The intermolecular force (both attractive and repulsive) are negligible except during collision 
  • 2. The volume of the molecules is negligible compared with the volume occupied by the gas
  • 3. Between collision a molecule moves with unifonn velocity 
  • 4. The duration of a collision is negligible compared with time between collisions. 

 

The molecules of a gas are all identical, the molecules of a gas collide with one another and with the walls of the container, as temperature increases, the number of collisions made by the gas molecules increase.
