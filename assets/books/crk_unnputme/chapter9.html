JAMB Example

1. Paul informed the Galatians that the gospel he preached came through

A. a revelation of Jesus Christ

B. his desire to lead them

C. inspiration from the angels

D. the faith of men.

Option A. 2010 

Reference: Galatians 1: 11 - 12  

[11] For I would have you know, brethren, that the gospel which was preached by me is not man's gospel.  [12] For I did not receive it from man, nor was I taught it, but it came through a revelation of Jesus Christ.

 

2. According to John, Jesus said the work of God is

A. preaching the gospel

B. healing the sick

C. feeding the hungry who are in need

D. believing in Him who he had sent.

Option D  

Reference: John 6: 29 

[29] Jesus answered them, "This is the work of God, that you believe in him whom he has sent."
