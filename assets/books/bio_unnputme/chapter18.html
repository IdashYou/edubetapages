METABOLISM 

The physical and chemical processes by which simple molecules of food are built up into complex molecules (assimilation, anabolism) and complex molecules are broken down into simple molecules (respiration) with liberation of energy for work by the organism (Note: Metabolism = Anabolism + Catabolism). Anabolism is an endothermic process since it absorbs energy. catabolism is an exothermic process since it releases energy.

PUTME Example

Which of the following is not true about anabolism? 

A. It is a building process B. It is an endothermic reaction C. It is an exothermic reaction D. It tends to the production of larger molecules 

(C. Anabolism is an endothermic process since it absorbs energy. catabolism is an exothermic process since it releases energy, 2017/2018, no 14)
