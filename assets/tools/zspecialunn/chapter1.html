STRUCTURE OF UNN PUTME SCREENING EXERCISE

The Exam is not Paper and Pen but CBT based just like your JAMB Exam. You don't have to come with any paper or calculator. You will only be allowed to come in with Your reprinted print-out containing the date and venue for your screening, and a pen if you need it.

Sometimes they provide a plane paper for those that might like to solve questions on a rough paper.

You will be writing 4 courses which is already selected for you. For each course you have 17 questions, and you are expected to answer any 15 from the 17.

NB: You cannot cancel any question you have answered, you can only change the option you selected in that particular number.

E.g.

Let's assume you answered from number 1 to 15, and on seeing number 16, you notice it is easier than the one you answered in number 1. At this point you can't unmark number 1 to answer number 16.

So, always avoid questions you are not sure of, since you are not expected to answer all. But always make sure you answered up to 15 Questions for each Course, unless they change the instruction.

Time limit: you have 1 hour to finish the four courses. That is 60minutes for 60 questions. With our App you can really master practically how to use your time, by enabling timer when practicing past questions.

4 NEW SUCCESS KEYS

It pricks our heart that the current resources (Books and Past Questions) available are not sufficient to prepare students for UNN PUTME exams. This motivated us to create this App.

This App is a product of Some Smart UNN Graduates, we actually conducted many researches before we discovered some untold secrets about UNN PUTME Screening.

This made us to come up with this app to enable the students to prepare better and smarter. We know you have Good textbooks and Past questions when we prepared the App. It is for those who want to fly higher.

Here are some secrets of success in the App, which will help you to perform excellently in the Exam hall.

  1. Extra Notes

We noticed that, there are questions that memorizing JAMB and PUTME Questions can't save you from. Don't get me wrong, normal Past questions are very important but not perfect. We have a better plan. We prepared UNN PUTME EXAM FOCUSED SUMMARY NOTES for the courses you will be writing. It is not just like normal notes in your textbooks. It is designed to cover all the possible questions that will come in each topic, and each topic is followed with PUTME and JAMB expected questions on that topic for better understanding. This is because reading a topic without being able to answer a question in that topic is useless. Don’t do blind reading during preparation, remember that your goal is to be able to answer questions, not just to understand the topic.

  1. Smart Covering of Past Questions

We also noticed that many students try to read every JAMB past question but this is really not smart because there is how the brain works. Things register in your brain faster when they are closely associated. That is why we grouped some expected JAMB and PUTME questions according to topic in our summary notes, with supporting explanations.

  1. Areas of Concentration

All topics are not read in the same proportion. Intact you will never see some topics that are in your textbook. We have identified areas of concentration in each topic in within the notes. So, pay attention to those points. You are free to finish what ever book you wish to finish but never play with the areas of concentration in the App’s note. Make sure you master all of them, and revise them few days before the exam.

  1. Practical Time Management

Time is a big issue. Most students end up not finishing their questions although they know the answers. This is because they just practiced with Past Questions. Time management may not look like a problem to you now, till you enter the hall. You might be distracted, maybe due to system issues. So, you need to really know how to finish as fast as possible. With our CBT past question in the App you can test yourself by enabling Timer while taking the test. Set the time to be less than the PUTME time to train your mind to be fast. It is like UNN CBT exam, with stop time and calculator. And you don't need Internet connection to use the App, so you can even put your phone in Airplane mode.
